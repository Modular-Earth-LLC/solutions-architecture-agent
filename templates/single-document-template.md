# Single Document Template

Use this template for Direct Delivery and Custom Document flows. All sections are optional — include only those relevant to the deliverable specification from scope negotiation.

## Document Structure

### Executive Summary (required)
- 1-2 paragraphs: problem, recommended approach, key benefits
- Audience: decision-makers who may read only this section
- Word budget: 150-300 words

### Current State Assessment (optional)
- Pain points, technical landscape, business impact
- Word budget: 200-400 words

### Proposed Solution (required)
- Architecture overview, technology recommendations, approach
- Include 1 Mermaid diagram (system context or data flow)
- Word budget: 300-600 words

### Implementation Approach (optional)
- Phased delivery, timeline, key milestones
- Word budget: 200-400 words

### Security and Compliance (optional)
- Top threats, compliance requirements, mitigation strategy
- Word budget: 150-300 words

### Cost and Investment (optional)
- T-shirt sizing, cost range, ROI summary
- Word budget: 150-300 words

### Risk and Assumptions (optional)
- Top 3-5 risks with mitigations, key assumptions
- Word budget: 100-200 words

### Recommendations and Next Steps (required)
- Clear action items, decision points, timeline
- Word budget: 100-200 words

## Guidelines

- Total target: 8-12 pages (2,400-3,600 words)
- Adjust word budgets proportionally if target differs
- Every claim must be justified — no filler or generic boilerplate
- Mermaid diagrams follow rules in `.claude/rules/mermaid-diagrams.md`
