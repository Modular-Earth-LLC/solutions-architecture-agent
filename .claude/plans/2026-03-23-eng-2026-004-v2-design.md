## Architecture Principles for v2

1. Assignment fidelity over comprehensiveness — answer exactly what's asked
2. Generic-first architecture — design the system, then choose the vendors
3. Progressive disclosure — every visual builds on the previous one
4. Single source of truth — one document grounds all others
5. Paul-speakable content only — if Paul can't confidently explain it, remove it
6. Cited or removed — every claim has a source, or it doesn't exist
7. AI-first, not AI-only — use traditional ML where it's the right tool
8. Earn trust through honesty — acknowledge unknowns as discovery questions
9. Simplicity is sophistication — fewest components that meet all requirements
10. Demo proves the vision — first phase of implementation, not a throwaway
